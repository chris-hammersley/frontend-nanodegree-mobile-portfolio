    Image not representative of app. I'm developing a mobile phone app for Safety Supervisors.     I'm creating a daily safety meeting tablet app that provides reporting for Construction Supervisors. Don't mind the image. The Safety Supervisor receives safety talking points to review with his/her work crew, shows a short video demonstrating safety techniques, snaps a picture of the work crew and then signs role call. All report data is synced with the company account in the LMS.
