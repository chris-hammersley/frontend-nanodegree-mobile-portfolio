  • What's UNSAFE Here?

    I'm working on a Hidden Object javascript/HTML canvas game that will be the landing page for an email & print campaign targeting the youth market.

  • Safety Tracker Mobile App

    I'm developing a mobile phone app for Safety Supervisors. Supervisors can snap a photo of a safety violation, scan a worker's hard hat barcode, identify the hazard and report the violation. The app integrates with the company account in the LMS and generates a daily report which recommends appropriate online safety courses for the worker.

  • Safety Role Call

    I'm creating a daily toolbox safety meeting app for tablets that provides compliance reporting for the Construction Foreman who holds safety meetings on site before work begins every morning. Foreman receives safety talking points to review with work crew, shows a short video demonstrating safety techniques, snaps a picture of the work crew and takes role call. All report data is synced with the company account in the LMS.

  • Your Safety QAD

    I'm building a mobile game for the construction worker. This game will engage the worker with a Safety Question-a-Day, explaining and reinforcing safety concepts that are instrumental in keeping workers safe on the job site. It will be tailored to the workers job and 30 days of correct answers wins a prize. Each month has a different theme that ties into an online course.
